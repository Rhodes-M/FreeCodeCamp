• Queen Elizabeth II was born on April 21, 1926, in London, England.
  • Queen Elizabeth II married Prince Philip, Duke of Edinburgh, on November 20, 1947.
  • She became the Queen of the United Kingdom and other Commonwealth realms on February 6, 1952.
  • During her reign, she witnessed significant historical events, including the end of World War II and the dissolution of the British Empire.
  • On 6 February 2022, Elizabeth II became the first British monarch to reign for 70 years.
  • Queen Elizabeth II has been a symbol of continuity and stability for the United Kingdom and the Commonwealth.
  • Her Majesty has undertaken numerous official duties and engagements, representing the monarchy globally.
  • Queen Elizabeth II sadly passed away at Balmoral Castle on 8 September 2022.
